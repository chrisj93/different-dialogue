Who we are


Partnership formed in 2022 by Bart De Miersman and William Brenn, two seasoned global sourcing executives who met while leading global supply chains for C&A.

Leadership team has over 100 years of experience combined in sourcing and managing production with brands ranging from luxury to value.

Categories including formalwear, sportswear, cotton basics and technical outerwear.


Different Dialogue was founded to achieve two goals:

1. Bridge the gap in sourcing and sustainability for medium to small brands.

2. Shift the narrative on global sourcing through strategic thought-leadership projects with partner brands.

We have Partner offices in China, Vietnam and Bangladesh as well as Customer Support offices in Europe and the United States.


What we offer


A one-stop complete sourcing solution with bespoke product development and procurement support.


End to End Complete Product and Sourcing Development: Fully Traceable, High Quality and Cost Competitive Product Sourcing.

Specific Point Partnership and Collaboration: Project based consultancy services that aim to drive best practice for Future Sourcing Landscapes.

Current Categories: Cotton Basics and Core Product, Athliesure and Equestrian Wear, Uniforms



How can we help?

Increase Revenues

  • Full suite of product development services to help you design, manufacture and bring to market bestselling items. Drive your quality and performance without sacrificing design.
  • Logistics and procurement support to help you expand your markets. From tax efficient sourcing locations to duty and import handling.
  • Cutting edge sustainable raw material development including - Regenerative cotton, circular polyester, bio-based synthetics.

Increase Profits

  • Cross quotation and supplier benchmarking to ensure you are paying accurate CoGs
  • Speed and replenishment models - We can structure the production schedule to maximise your decision windows. Giving you time to read the market and react to bestsellers - driving full price sell through and profit.

Reduce Risk

  • Sourcing Risk Management - We can help you analyze your current sourcing COO landscape. This helps ensure that you are not over-exposed in any one Country of Origin, from a garment and a fabric side.
    • Develop alternative markets in cases where you are exposed - thereby giving you options and potentially cost saving
    • Regulatory and Legislative risk - China Tariffs, EU Due Diligence and NY Fashion Act. All present major risk to brands - we can help you navigate this.
  • Enhance traceability to ensure quality and compliance. Leverage Different Dialogues scale with technological solutions such as Fibretrace and Hyran to drive end to end traceability and reduce risk
